Pasta Recipe

Description

A nutritious bowl of Marinara Pasta and how to make it

Ingredients

  • A box of pasta
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Salt
  • Additional Seasoning (Optional)
  • Chopped Vegetables (Optional)

Recipe Instructions

  1. Boil Pasta for 6-8 mins
  2. While Pasta is boiling add olive oil and marinara sauce to pan and heat under light flame
  3. When pasta is done boiling, drain water and add boiled pasta onto the frying pan
  4. Add salt, additional seasoning and vegetables while sauteing pasta
  5. After 1-2 mins of light sauteing, serve pasta in bowl or plate
